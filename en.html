Internal Newsletter – April 2023
A Message from the Director General
    Dear Abu Dhabi Chamber family,

    I would like to start by welcoming you to the first edition of our internal newsletter, which will keep you informed about the Chamber’s latest developments, achievements, announcements, and internal activities, as well as our people’s successes.
    Despite the shift in the global economic landscape and the challenges that we faced during the past year, we were able to realise remarkable achievements. We started by enhancing our services and announcing our new organisational structure. Then we launched our new strategy, which centres on ensuring the flexibility of our business model, meeting the demands of the business community, and empowering the private sector, to contribute to the competitiveness of our local economy and attract foreign direct investments.

    These achievements would not have been possible without your exceptional dedication, tireless efforts, and unwavering commitment towards our vision of a bright future. Your contributions have been a major driver towards achieving our collective goals and overcoming obstacles. You are the unknown soldiers who work tirelessly behind the scenes, to deliver services and solutions that meet the needs of our members, both locally and globally. Your creativity, innovation, and passion for excellence are an inspiration to us all.

    We have a bright and hopeful view of what lies ahead. Let us work together with utmost motivation to achieve the aspirations of the Emirate of Abu Dhabi and the UAE, following our wise leadership’s vision of driving the country’s comprehensive development to establish the UAE’s position as a leader across different industries.

    Mohamed Helal Al Mheiri

Preparedness training and exercises
On Tuesday, 21 March 2023, the Abu Dhabi Chamber conducted a successful evacuation mock drill in the main building of the Abu Dhabi Chamber, as part of its health and safety activities. This reflect the Chamber's efforts to ensure its employees wellbeing and safety, to save lives and property.


The drill aimed at training employees on the best ways to deal with crises, including fires, effectively. It also familiarised them with the designated exits and assembly points in order to avoid stampedes and injuries.
Did you know?
Interesting fact about the UAE
  • Did you know that the street names in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi have significant cultural, historical and social meanings?

    We often wonder what is behind street names.

    For example, “Al Quffal” is a street name, which in Arabic means coming back after a diving trip. For centuries, diving has been a major aspect of the economic life of the coastal communities in the UAE.
    • Interesting fact about the Abu Dhabi Chamber
  • Did you know that the first headquarters of the Abu Dhabi Chamber, which was established in 1969, was an apartment consisting of two rooms in a building on Sheikh Khalifa Street, and was occupied by only one employee?



Talk Wellness
Exercising is important for maintaining physical and mental wellness and improving blood circulation. Below is a list of the most common workouts.
1
Running: Running improves cardiovascular fitness and strengthens your muscles. Running can be practiced in designated sports areas, sports clubs, or outdoors.
2
Walking: Walking is important for maintaining a healthy body and preventing various conditions like heart disease, stroke, and more.
3
Low impact cardio exercises: These exercises work all your muscles and maintain flexibility. They are important for reducing body fat.
4
Activities and Hobbies: Sports activities, such as cycling and swimming, improve circulation, build endurance, and improve overall health and fitness.
Stay informed
Cybersecurity is important for protecting the systems and data of government and private entities from cyber attacks. Smartphones have become a major part of our lives, and the most common way to use the Internet. If smartphones are not well protected, they could be prone to vulnerabilities, including data theft.
Following the steps below will reduce the risk of
experiencing a cyberattack:
- Update your mobile’s operating system
- Do not allow permission for all applications, if not required
- Store sensitive information in a secured area
- Do not connect to public Wi-Fi networks
- Install reliable security software
